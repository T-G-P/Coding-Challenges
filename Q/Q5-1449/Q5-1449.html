Q5

Country of origin:
United States
Location:
Seattle, Washington
Status:
Split-up
Formed in:
1983
Genre:
Heavy Metal/Hard Rock
Lyrical themes:
Women, Love, Life, War
Last label:
Unsigned/independent
Years active:
1983-1986, 2009
The band is effectively members of TKO who were won over by the strength of Floyd Rose's compositions.

After Floyd Rose decided to quit, the remaining members formed Nightshade.

Briefly reunited to play Headbangers Open Air in 2009.

Floyd Rose is the inventor of the famous locking tremolo for guitars.
