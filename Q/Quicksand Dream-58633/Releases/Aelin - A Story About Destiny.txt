	            <div class="ui-tabs-panel-content  block_spacer_top_20">
	              <p class="block_spacer_20"><i>Aelin</i> is a full concept album which was released in CD-R format by the band (only 30 copies complete with a colorful homemade 16-page booklet).<br />
<br />
"The Lighthouse Dream" consists of 6 parts: <br />
a) Fading Away<br />
b) On the Shore<br />
c) A Sunny Day<br />
d) Over the Sea<br />
e) Awakening<br />
f) Home Again<br />
<br />
"Aelin's Oath" consists of 2 parts: <br />
a) The Light Appeared <br />
b) Epilogue</p>
				  					<p class="title_comment">Recording information:</p>
					<p class="block_spacer_20">The music and lyrics are written by P. Backlund, except the music on The Lighthouse Dream (Fading Away, On the Shore, A Sunny Day and Home Again), and the lyrics on Road Goes Ever On and The Lighthouse Dream, which are based on earlier versions written by P. Backlund and M. Svedlund. All vocal lines and additional material by G. Jacobson.<br />
<br />
Recorded 1997-1999 by P. Backlund, except the keyboards and vocals, which were recorded by P. Backlund, G. Jacobson and H. Flyman.<br />
Mixed and mastered by P. Backlund, G. Jacobson and H. Flyman.<br />
Layout by Quicksand Dream and Scott Hoffman / Eyedolatry.</p>
