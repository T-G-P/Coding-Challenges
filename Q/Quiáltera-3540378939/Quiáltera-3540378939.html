Quiáltera is a brazilian Prog Metal/Prog Rock band formed in 2011 by Yuri Deryous, who was bassist at the time, with the drummer Emerson Sant'anna and Victor Paron and Vinicius Sant'anna on guitars. That same year they released a demo called “Bicho Instrumental” containing some interpretations of varied instrumental songs, plus two original compositions.
